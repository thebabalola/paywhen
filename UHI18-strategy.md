## 🪝 Technical Architecture (ForgeX + Vult)
- **Problem**: In standard AMMs, liquidity is "lazy" and only earns fees when someone swaps.
- **Solution**: A **"Yield-Compounding" hook** that moves idle pool liquidity into ForgeX ERC-4626 vaults, earning **Stablecoin Yield** on top of **Trading Fees**.
- **The "Winner" Logic**: Use the `afterSwap` hook to harvest interest and donate it back to active Liquidity Providers (LPs).
